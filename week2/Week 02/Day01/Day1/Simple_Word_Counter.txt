Input: paragraph of text

Method wordCounter(text)
    Convert text to lowercase
    Remove punctuation
    Split text into words
    Initialize Map<String, Integer> wordCount
    For each word in words
        If word exists in map → increment count
        Else add word with count = 1
    Sort map by frequency
    Print words with frequency

MAIN:
    paragraph = "The news today is about the rise in technology trends."
    wordCounter(paragraph)

Output:
    the → 2
    news → 1
    today → 1
    is → 1
    about → 1
    rise → 1
    in → 1
    technology → 1
    trends → 1
