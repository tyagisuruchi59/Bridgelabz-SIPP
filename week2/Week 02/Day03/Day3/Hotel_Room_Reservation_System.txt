Pseudo-code: Hotel Room Reservation System

Classes:
1. Room (Base Class)
   - Attributes: roomNumber, customerName, daysStayed
   - Methods: calculateBill() → overridden

2. DeluxeRoom (Subclass of Room)
   - Rate: ₹3000 per night
   - calculateBill() → daysStayed * 3000

3. SuiteRoom (Subclass of Room)
   - Rate: ₹5000 per night
   - calculateBill() → daysStayed * 5000

Booking Flow:
- Ravi books Deluxe for 3 nights → Bill ₹9000
- Anita books Suite for 2 nights → Bill ₹10000
- Print invoice at checkout.
