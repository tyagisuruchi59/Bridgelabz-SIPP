Pseudo-code: Hospital Department Mapping

Class Doctor:
- Attributes: name, specialization, experienceYears

Data Structure:
- Map<String, List<Doctor>> departmentDirectory

Operations:
- addDoctor(department, doctor)
- getDoctors(department)

Example Data:
Cardiology → [Dr. Mehta (15 yrs), Dr. Kapoor (10 yrs)]
Neurology → [Dr. Sharma (10 yrs), Dr. Das (8 yrs)]

Query: "Neurology" → List doctors in Neurology
