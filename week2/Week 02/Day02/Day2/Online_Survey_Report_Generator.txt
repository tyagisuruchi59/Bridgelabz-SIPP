Class SurveyResponse
    Attributes: restaurantName, rating

Method generateReport(responses)
    Use groupingBy(restaurantName, averagingInt(rating))
    For each restaurant → compute average rating
    Print report

    Find max average → Best restaurant
    Find min average → Needs improvement

MAIN:
    responses = [
        ("PizzaHut", 4), ("PizzaHut", 5),
        ("KFC", 3), ("KFC", 4),
        ("Subway", 5), ("Subway", 4), ("Subway", 5)
    ]

    generateReport(responses)

Output:
    PizzaHut → 4.5
    KFC → 3.5
    Subway → 4.67
    Best = Subway
    Needs improvement = KFC
